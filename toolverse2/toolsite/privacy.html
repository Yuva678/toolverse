Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025. This policy explains how ToolVerse handles information.

🔒 Short version: All ToolVerse tools run in your browser. Your text, images, and data are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store your input.

1. Information We Collect

ToolVerse is committed to your privacy. The tools on this website operate entirely within your web browser. This means any text you type or paste, and any images you upload, are processed locally and never sent to our servers. We do not store, log, or transmit your tool inputs or outputs.

We may automatically collect non-personal technical information when you visit our site, including browser type, operating system, referring URL, and pages visited. This is used solely for improving website performance.

2. Cookies

ToolVerse uses minimal cookies: your dark/light mode preference is stored in localStorage on your device and never transmitted. We may use Google Analytics for anonymous aggregate usage data. We use Google AdSense which may set cookies to serve relevant advertisements.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

ToolVerse displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts — fonts loaded from Google's CDN.
  • Google Analytics — anonymised traffic analysis.
  • Google AdSense — displaying relevant advertisements.

5. Data Security

Because our tools process data entirely in your browser and we do not collect personal input data, privacy risk is minimal. We implement HTTPS encryption on all pages.

6. Children's Privacy

ToolVerse does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us for deletion.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are indicated by updating the date above. Continued use of ToolVerse constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us.
