📖 How to Use Age Calculator

  1. Select your Date of Birth using the date picker.
  2. Optionally change the "Calculate Age At" date (defaults to today).
  3. Click Calculate Age to see your exact age in all units.
  4. Your next birthday countdown is shown at the bottom.

ℹ️ About Age Calculator

This tool accurately calculates age accounting for leap years and varying month lengths. It can also calculate age at any past or future date — useful for historical research, planning, or checking ages at specific events. All calculations happen instantly in your browser.
