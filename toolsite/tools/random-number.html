Random Number Generator

Free

Generate truly random numbers within any range. Choose a single number or generate multiple unique values. Useful for games, lotteries, statistics, and random sampling.

⚡ Quick Presets

📖 How to Use

  1. Set your Minimum and Maximum range values.
  2. Choose how many random numbers to generate (1–1000).
  3. Toggle options: No duplicates, Sort results, or Allow decimals.
  4. Click Generate — numbers appear instantly. Press Enter for quick generation.

ℹ️ About Random Number Generator

Uses JavaScript's Math.random() which produces cryptographically-adequate pseudo-random numbers for most purposes including games, raffles, sampling, and statistical exercises. For security-critical applications, use a dedicated cryptographic random source.
