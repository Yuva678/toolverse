📖 How to Convert PNG to JPG

  1. Upload a PNG image by dropping it or clicking the upload area.
  2. Adjust JPEG quality (90% recommended for high quality).
  3. Set a background color — this fills transparent areas in the PNG.
  4. Click Download JPG to save the converted file.

ℹ️ PNG vs JPG

JPG files are much smaller than PNG for photographs. Converting PNG to JPG reduces file size significantly, making images faster to load online. The trade-off is slight quality loss and no transparency support. JPG quality of 80–90% is generally indistinguishable from the original.
