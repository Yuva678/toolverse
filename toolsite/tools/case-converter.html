📖 How to Use Case Converter

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box.
  2. Click any case button (e.g. UPPERCASE, Title Case) to convert instantly.
  3. The result appears in the output box. Click Copy to copy it.
  4. Use snake_case and kebab-case for programming variable names.

ℹ️ About Case Converter

This free online case converter supports 10 different text cases. It's ideal for programmers naming variables, writers standardising headings, or anyone who needs quick text transformations. All processing happens in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.
