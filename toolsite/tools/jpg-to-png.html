ℹ️ JPG vs PNG

PNG is a lossless format that preserves every pixel perfectly and supports transparency. It's ideal for logos, icons, screenshots, and images with text. JPG is better for photographs where smaller file size matters more than perfect quality. Convert to PNG when you need the highest fidelity copy of an image.
