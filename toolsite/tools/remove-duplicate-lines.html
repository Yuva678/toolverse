📖 How to Use

  1. Paste your text (one item per line) into the input area.
  2. Choose options: Trim whitespace, Case-insensitive matching, Remove blank lines.
  3. Click Remove Duplicates to process your text.
  4. Copy the cleaned result to your clipboard.

ℹ️ About This Tool

Ideal for cleaning up email lists, deduplicating keywords, tidying CSV exports, or removing repeated entries from log files. Case-insensitive mode treats "Apple" and "apple" as duplicates. Completely private — your text never leaves your browser.
