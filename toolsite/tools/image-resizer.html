📖 How to Resize an Image

  1. Drop an image into the upload area or click to browse your files.
  2. Choose By Pixels for exact dimensions or By Percentage to scale proportionally.
  3. Enter your width and/or height. Enable Maintain aspect ratio to avoid distortion.
  4. Select output format (PNG, JPEG, or WebP) and click Resize & Download.
