📖 How to Compress Images

  1. Upload an image by dropping it or clicking the upload area.
  2. Adjust the Quality slider — 70–80% is a good balance for most images.
  3. Choose output format: JPEG or WebP gives the best compression.
  4. Click Compress Image, review the size savings, then download.

ℹ️ About Image Compressor

Smaller images load faster and use less storage. This tool is ideal for optimising website images, reducing email attachment sizes, or fitting images within upload limits. WebP format typically achieves 25–35% smaller files than JPEG at equivalent quality.
